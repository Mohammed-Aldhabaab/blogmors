What is the Point of Life?!

Finding purpose in life is one of those things that most people want. Whether or not we realize it. Even while it sounds lovely, achieving it might seem difficult.

If you haven't given your personal purpose much thought, you could have some preconceived notions about what life is all about. These preconceived notions about life are frequently shaped by our upbringing in our families and communities. Like religious, to get married and to raise children, or perhaps it's obtaining a specific financial goal or standing in society …etc.

However, these kinds of successes frequently don't result in the kind of pleasure that comes from discovering your own sense of purpose or might not be the truth.

Your own sense of purpose serves as both a compass and a foundation. across the years and from day to day. Purpose provides you with stability and a feeling of direction even when things go wrong and the world seems to be turned upside down. Finding meaning is crucial to have a happy, fulfilling life because of this.

Even if it can sound high to ask what your purpose is, it's an important question to ask. and attempting to respond. Finding your purpose can unlock greater satisfaction and success in all areas of your life.

So let's get to the major issues: what is life's purpose and why does it matter?

Why is finding purpose important?

Finding your life's purpose may seem like a nice-to-have, but it's more crucial than you would realize.

A meaningful existence makes motivated toward your life, happier, lowers the chance of developing chronic diseases, promotes improved physical and mental fitness ...etc. You may even live longer, according to several research

If you did not find your purpose, your purpose will undoubtedly change during the course of your life. And might cause you some problems in your life!

What is the purpose of life?

Most individuals eventually wonder, "Why am I here?" It may be a frightening or exciting question. Leaning into the discomfort of our existence can lead to a more positive sense of self and more meaningful future possibilities...
